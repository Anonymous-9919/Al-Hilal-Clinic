Comprehensive Face & Skin Treatments

Every treatment is performed by our expert medical team using premium products and the latest techniques for natural, lasting results.

Botox & Dysport

Botox and Dysport are the world's leading neuromodulator treatments for reducing the appearance of dynamic wrinkles. By temporarily relaxing the underlying facial muscles, these injections smooth moderate to severe frown lines, crow's feet, forehead wrinkles and bunny lines. Results appear within days and last 3–4 months, leaving you with a naturally refreshed and youthful expression. At Al Hilal Clinic, our specialists use precise techniques to ensure subtle, beautiful results every time.

  • Forehead lines & frown lines
  • Crow's feet & under-eye wrinkles
  • Bunny lines on the nose
  • Lip lines & gummy smile
  • Non-surgical brow lift

Masseter Botox

Masseter Botox involves targeted injections into the masseter muscles of the jaw to slim the lower face and relieve teeth grinding (bruxism). This popular treatment softens a square jawline, creating a more V-shaped, feminine or balanced facial contour. It also alleviates tension headaches, jaw clenching and TMJ discomfort. The effects develop over 2–4 weeks and typically last 4–6 months.

  • Jaw slimming & V-shape contouring
  • Teeth grinding (bruxism) relief
  • TMJ pain & tension headaches
  • Facial asymmetry correction
  • Non-surgical face slimming

High Potency Skin Booster

High Potency Skin Boosters are advanced injectable treatments that deliver a powerful blend of hyaluronic acid, amino acids, vitamins and antioxidants deep into the dermis. Unlike superficial moisturisers, these boosters hydrate the skin from within, stimulating collagen production and improving elasticity. The result is a radiant, plump, dewy complexion with reduced fine lines and improved skin texture. A course of 2–3 sessions is recommended for optimal, long-lasting results.

  • Deep hydration & skin plumping
  • Collagen & elastin stimulation
  • Fine line & wrinkle reduction
  • Improved skin texture & radiance
  • Dull, tired & dehydrated skin

Nefertiti Lift (Neck & Jawline)

The Nefertiti Lift is a non-surgical neck and jawline rejuvenation technique using strategic Botox injections. By relaxing the platysma bands (the vertical neck muscles) and the depressor muscles pulling down the jaw, the treatment creates a visible lift and tightening effect. It defines the jawline, reduces neck banding and softens marionette lines, giving a more sculpted profile. The procedure takes under 20 minutes with no downtime and results last 3–4 months.

  • Platysma band reduction
  • Jawline definition & lift
  • Neck tightening & contouring
  • Marionette line softening
  • Non-surgical lower face lift

Hyperhidrosis Treatment

Hyperhidrosis (excessive sweating) can be effectively treated with Botox injections in the underarms, palms, soles or forehead. Botox blocks the chemical signals that stimulate sweat glands, significantly reducing perspiration in the treated area. The treatment is quick, minimally invasive and provides relief for 6–12 months. Many patients report a life-changing improvement in comfort and confidence after just one session.

  • Excessive underarm sweating
  • Palmar hyperhidrosis (sweaty palms)
  • Plantar hyperhidrosis (sweaty feet)
  • Facial blushing & sweating
  • Long-lasting sweat reduction (6–12 months)

Eyelid Lifting

Non-surgical eyelid lifting uses targeted Botox injections around the brow and eye area to open up the eyes and create a more alert, youthful appearance. By relaxing the muscles that pull the brows down and the orbicularis oculi, the eyebrows lift subtly and crow's feet are smoothed. The treatment can also address drooping upper eyelids and asymmetry. Results are visible within a week and last 3–4 months with zero downtime.

  • Drooping upper eyelids
  • Brow ptosis (low brows)
  • Periorbital fine lines & crow's feet
  • Asymmetric eye appearance
  • Tired, heavy-looking eyes

Neck Tightening

Neck tightening treatments combine laser, radiofrequency or injectable therapies to firm and rejuvenate loose, sagging skin on the neck and décolletage. These non-invasive modalities stimulate collagen and elastin remodelling, improving skin laxity and reducing the appearance of horizontal neck lines (tech neck). Patients enjoy a smoother, more contoured neck profile without surgery or extended recovery. A series of sessions is typically recommended for best outcomes.

  • Loose, sagging neck skin
  • Horizontal neck lines (tech neck)
  • Loss of jawline definition
  • Turkey neck / double chin
  • Non-invasive skin tightening

Nasolabial Folds Treatment

Nasolabial folds (the lines running from the nose to the corners of the mouth) deepen with age as facial fat pads descend and collagen is lost. Dermal filler injections safely soften these folds by restoring volume and support to the midface. The result is a smoother, more rested appearance without looking overfilled. Our practitioners use gentle techniques and premium fillers to achieve beautiful, lasting correction.

  • Deep nasolabial folds (smile lines)

Radiesse

It provides immediate volume correction while stimulating your own collagen production for continued improvement over time. Radiesse is particularly effective for deeper wrinkles, cheek augmentation, jawline contouring and hand rejuvenation. Results last 12–18 months or longer, making it a cost-effective choice for long-term facial rejuvenation.

Radiofrequency Microneedling

Radiofrequency (RF) microneedling combines the collagen-induction power of microneedling with the skin-tightening energy of radiofrequency. Ultra-fine needles penetrate the dermis and deliver controlled RF heat, stimulating deep collagen remodelling while protecting the skin's surface. This technology is highly effective for skin tightening, scar revision, pore refinement and laxity correction. Most patients see noticeable improvement after a single session, with optimal results after 2–3 treatments.

Mesotherapy for Fat & Tone

Mesotherapy involves micro-injections of a custom blend of vitamins, enzymes, amino acids and lipolytic compounds directly into the mesoderm (middle layer of skin). This treatment targets localised fat deposits, cellulite and skin laxity while simultaneously nourishing and toning the treated area. Commonly treated areas include the face, abdomen, thighs and arms. A course of 4–6 weekly sessions delivers progressive fat reduction and improved skin firmness.

Lab Diagnostics for Hair Loss

Comprehensive laboratory diagnostics identify the root causes of hair loss, enabling targeted, effective treatment. Results are interpreted by our specialists who create a personalised treatment plan addressing your specific hair loss type. This synergistic treatment dramatically improves scar appearance, reduces pigmentation, tightens pores and restores skin radiance. A course of 3 sessions yields exceptional, cumulative results.

Alopecia Areata Treatment

Alopecia areata is an autoimmune condition causing patchy hair loss that can affect the scalp, eyebrows, beard and body. Our clinic offers advanced treatment options including topical immunotherapy, corticosteroid injections, PRP therapy and JAK inhibitors to suppress the immune attack and promote hair regrowth. Each treatment plan is individualised based on the severity and pattern of hair loss. Early intervention offers the best prognosis for full recovery.

  • Patchy scalp hair loss
  • Eyebrow & beard alopecia
