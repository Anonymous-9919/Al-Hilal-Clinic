Radiofrequency Microneedling

Radiofrequency (RF) microneedling combines the collagen-induction power of microneedling with the skin-tightening energy of radiofrequency. Ultra-fine needles penetrate the dermis and deliver controlled RF heat, stimulating deep collagen remodelling while protecting the skin’s surface. This technology is highly effective for skin tightening, scar revision, pore refinement and laxity correction. Most patients see noticeable improvement after a single session, with optimal results after 2–3 treatments.

About Radiofrequency Microneedling

Radiofrequency (RF) microneedling combines the collagen-induction power of microneedling with the skin-tightening energy of radiofrequency. Key Benefits

  • Skin laxity & sagging
  • Acne scars & boxcar scars
  • Enlarged pores & textural issues
  • Fine lines & jowls
  • Non-surgical face & neck tightening
